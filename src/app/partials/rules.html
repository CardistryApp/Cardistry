Welcome to Cards Against Singularity!

A single player adaptation of everyone's favorite raunchy card game.

If you've never played before, just think mad-libs with a foul mouth!

There are two modes of play in Cards Against Singularity: Player and Czar

In Player Mode, you have 10 white 'answer' cards in your hand

Cycle through the cards and find the best answer to fill in the blank!

Select your favorite answer and that Question/Answer combo is removed from your hand and submitted to the Czar pool

You are given another question card and a new answer card for your hand!

In Czar mode, you hold the power to pad someone's ego or crush their spirits! (some results may vary)

As Czar, you work your way through the pool of user submitted cards.

If you find it funny, give the user that submitted it a point

Not so funny? Deny the point and imagine their sad faces!

So that's pretty much it! Press "Deal Me In" below to get your hand and remember:

Laughing at this game doesn't make you a horrible person... all the other stuff you do, however, is questionable
